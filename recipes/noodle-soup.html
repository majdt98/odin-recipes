Noodle soup

Description

This one-pot recipe for Quick & Easy Chinese Noodle Soup makes a super simple, aromatic broth thatâ€™s packed with noodles and Asian flavor.

Serve this soup as a starter for Chicken and Broccoli or Chicken and Chinese Mushrooms for an authentic Chinese meal right in your own kitchen!

Ingredients

  • chicken stock
  • green onions
  • bok choy or chinese greens
  • ligh and dark soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • noodles

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the chicken stock and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the green onions.
  3. Add the Bok Choy or Chinese greens
  4. Add light and dark soy sauce
  5. Add the oyster sauce
  6. Add the noodles right into the soup
  7. Follow the instructions on your noodle package for cooking time
  8. Once cooked serve and enjoy!
